Is Ciena's pay range off-market for Ottawa — and if so, by how much?
Revelio Labs (140 Ottawa salary samples, 11 companies, 4+ samples per company required) · Nokia from Glassdoor Dec 2025 · Telecom carriers excluded
!
Yes — the top of Ciena's range is below market. The starting pay ($115K) is competitive. But the maximum Ciena can offer ($149,400) is below the average pay at 6 of 12 Ottawa companies in this analysis.
Why these 12 companies were selected for this analysis
This analysis includes only semiconductor and optical networking companies with an Ottawa office — the same labour market Ciena draws from for R026772. All 12 companies design or manufacture hardware that requires expertise in the same technical areas this role needs (optical modules, high-speed electrical design, DSP, or embedded systems). Telecom service providers such as TELUS and Rogers were excluded because they hire for different roles and do not compete for the same engineering profiles. Only companies with 4 or more Ottawa salary samples in the Revelio Labs dataset were included to ensure the averages are reliable, not based on one or two data points. Nokia (9 samples, Glassdoor Dec 2025) was added because it is Ciena's most direct Ottawa competitor. ARM was reviewed but had no Ottawa-specific salary data in Revelio — their professionals in this dataset are based in Toronto and Austin, not Ottawa. Credo Semiconductor had only 2 Ottawa samples, below the 4-sample minimum required for inclusion.

The direct answer
?Is Ciena's range off-market — and by how much?
Is it off-market?
At the top, yes
The starting pay ($115K) and midpoint ($132K) are in line with Ottawa's market average of $131,175. The issue is the maximum ($149,400) — it is below what 6 of 12 Ottawa companies pay their people on average.
By how much?
Up to $59,700
Intel's Ottawa average is $209,089 — $59,700 above Ciena's maximum. More relevantly, Synopsys (54 salary samples analyzed) pays an Ottawa average of $139,620 — meaning Ciena's maximum of $149,400 is only a 7% raise for someone leaving Synopsys. That is typically not enough to change jobs.
Who can Ciena not match?
6 of 12 companies
Intel, Nokia, Infineon, Marvell, Synopsys, and Cadence all pay more on average than Ciena's maximum. Someone at any of these companies would be taking a pay cut to join Ciena — even at the top of the range.

Recommendation
Raise the maximum — the starting pay can stay
The starting pay of $115,000 is reasonable and does not need to change. The problem is the maximum of $149,400 — it is below the average pay at 6 Ottawa competitors, which limits who Ciena can make a competitive offer to. Raising the maximum to $168K–$175K would put Ciena above all Ottawa companies in this dataset except Intel — and make competitive offers possible for experienced candidates from Synopsys, Marvell, and Infineon. If a range change is not approved, a one-time signing bonus of $15K–$25K can bridge the gap for specific candidates above the current maximum.
